Pluperfect Tense Plusquamperfekt

User to descrip something that had happened, or had been true at a point in the past. Often recognised in English by the word "had". For example: I had forgotten to brush my teeth.

Constructed from Hilfsverb im Präteritum and Partizip II.

Beispeile

  • Wir waren schon gegessen.
  • Ich hatte schon das Buch gelesen.

Plusquamperfekt is always used when referring to an event in the past that happened before another event in the past.

The event that came first uses Plusquamperfekt, and the event after that uses Präteritum or Perfekt.

Beispeile

  • Nachdem er das Ticket gekauft hatte, ist er nach Amerika geflogen.
