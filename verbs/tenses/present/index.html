Present Tense

There are two forms in English: one for things happening right now, and one for things that happen all the time. German uses the same for both.

Things happening now:

  • Es schneit.
  • Sie spielen Hockey.

Things that happen all the time:

  • Hier schneit es viel.
  • Freitags spielen wir Hockey.

In both English and German, you can use the present tense to talk about something that will happen in the near future:

  • Morgen fliege ich nach London.
  • Wir verlassen um 18 Uhr.
